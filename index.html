Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Want to Know

What is a Property Check?

A Property Check is a one-time, in-person visit where a property specialist inspects your home inside and out, documents everything with photos and video, and delivers a full report.

How quickly can someone visit my property?

Same-day and next-day visits are often available depending on your area — reach out and we'll confirm the soonest opening.

Can I schedule recurring visits?

Yes. Our Property Care plans include monthly or bi-weekly visits so your property is checked on a consistent schedule.

Can you check my house after a hurricane?

Yes — storm-related checks are one of the most common reasons owners call us. We prioritize post-storm visits whenever possible.

Do you work with real estate investors?

Yes. Many of our clients are investors who need eyes on vacant or tenant-free properties between showings, renovations, or closings.

Do you provide videos, not just photos?

Every Property Check includes an HD video walkthrough in addition to 40+ photos.

Can you meet a contractor at my property?

Yes, we can coordinate contractor access as part of a Property Check or ongoing Property Care plan.

Do you collect mail and packages?

Yes, mail and package checks are included in every Property Check visit.

What areas of Florida do you serve?

We currently serve counties along Florida's Gulf and East Coasts — see our full Service Area section above.

Is Acorn Property Group insured?

Insured coverage is coming soon as we build out our service area. We're happy to share our current status when you reach out.

Do you offer property management?

Full-service property management is coming soon and will only be offered once properly licensed or through a licensed Florida broker. In the meantime, our Property Checks and Property Care plans keep your home protected.

What's included in a property report?

A cover page, 40+ photos, a full Property Status Report, an HD video walkthrough, a written summary, and an overall property status.

Can you check on a vacant or unoccupied home?

Yes — vacant and vacant-land properties are one of our most common visit types.

How do I know if something is wrong with my property?

Your report will flag any issues found — leaks, HVAC problems, exterior damage, or anything else — along with clear recommendations.

Do you coordinate lawn care, pool service, or cleaning?

Yes. We can coordinate lawn, pool, cleaning, pest control, repairs, and HVAC service so you don't have to manage multiple vendors.

What happens if you find an emergency issue?

We contact you immediately with photos and next steps, and can coordinate emergency contractor access if needed.

Can second-home or seasonal owners use this service year-round?

Yes, many of our clients use us specifically during the months they're away from their Florida property.

Do realtors use Acorn Property Group?

Yes — realtors use us to keep listed or vacant properties presentable and to get alerted to any issues before showings.

Can you assist with probate or estate properties?

Yes, we regularly support estate representatives who need a local, trustworthy set of eyes on a property during the probate process.

How do I get started?

Schedule your first Property Check online, and a specialist will confirm your visit and follow up with your report within 24 hours.
